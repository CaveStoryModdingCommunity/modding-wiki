**OrgMaker 1 (usually refers to version 1.3.4)** is a program for creating and editing music in the [Organya format](organya), which is the music format used in Cave Story. It was developed by Pixel and supports up to 16 Tracks with 8 being dedicated to Melody and the other 8 being dedicated to Drums.

## Background
Development of OrgMaker 1 began in the summer of 1999 as an improved version of Pixel's previous music editor, Piyo Piyo[^1].

The first public build of version **1.0** dates back to October 19, 1999.

Since then, several updates have been released, and in the spring of 2003, the latest version **1.3.4** was released, in which all the music for Cave Story was created.
